IS 101: Advanced Presentation and Reasoning

Subject Meaning and Importance

Meaning

This subject develops higher-level communication and reasoning skills. It teaches how to structure arguments, support ideas with evidence, and deliver information persuasively and logically.

Importance

  • Improves communication skills for academic and professional settings.
  • Builds confidence in articulating and defending ideas.
  • Sharpens logical thinking and decision-making abilities.
  • Prepares for workplace presentations, meetings, and negotiations.
  • Strengthens leadership skills by enhancing persuasion and influence.
Example: Presenting a new IT system to non-technical managers by framing the problem, outlining clear options, and recommending the best solution with evidence.
